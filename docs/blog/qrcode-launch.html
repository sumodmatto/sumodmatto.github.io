New QR Code Generator App Launched!

We are excited to announce the launch of our new QR Code Generator App on Sumodmatto Pages. This tool allows you to create QR codes easily with various customization options.

The QR Code Generator App includes the following features:

  • Generate QR codes from text
  • Customize size and error correction levels
  • Preview generated QR codes directly on the page

We hope this tool will be useful for your projects and we are looking forward to adding more features in the future. Check it out now by visiting the QR Code Generator App page!

Stay tuned for more updates and new features. Thank you for being a part of the Sumodmatto Pages community.
